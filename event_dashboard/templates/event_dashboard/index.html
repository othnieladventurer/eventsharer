Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to use Eventsharer?

Our basic features are completely free to use forever. We also offer premium plans with additional storage and features starting at just $4.99/month.

Can I make my event private?

Absolutely! When creating your event, you can choose to make it public, private but accessible via link, or private with invitation-only access.

How long are photos and videos stored?

All your content is stored indefinitely in free accounts, but we recommend downloading backups. Premium accounts get enhanced backup options.

Can I download a full album?

Yes! With Eventsharer you can download all photos and videos from an event in one zip file, or select individual photos to download.

What's the file size limit for uploads?

Photos can be up to 30MB each. Videos have a limit of 5 minutes or 500MB, whichever comes first. Premium accounts get higher limits.
